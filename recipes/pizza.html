Gourmet Chicken Pizza

Home

Description

Gourmet Chicken Pizza is a mouthwatering fusion of tender chicken,gooey chicken, and vibrant flavors. With a crispy crust as the base, this pizza is topped with grilled chicken, a rich tomato or creamy white sauce, and an assortment of fresh toppings such as roasted bell peppers, caramelized onions, and a sprinkle of fresh herbs. Perfect for weeknight dinners or special occassions, this recipe offers a balance of savory and aromatic flavors that everyone will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 (10 ounce) can refigerated pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup Ranch-style salad dressing
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions

Steps

  1. Step 1 Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Lightly grease a pizza pan or medium baking sheet.
  2. Step 2 Place chicken in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook until no longer pink, and juices run clear. Cool, then either shred or chop into small pieces.
  3. Step 3 Unroll dough, and press into the prepared pizza pan or baking sheet. Bake crust for 7 minutes in the preheated oven, or until it begins to turn golden brown. Remove from oven.
  4. Step 4 Spread ranch dressing over partially baked crust. Sprinkle on monzzarella cheese. Place tomatoes, green onion, and chicken on top of mozzarella cheese, then top with Cheddar cheese. Return to the oven for 20 to 25 minutes, until cheese is melted and bubbly.
